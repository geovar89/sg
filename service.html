Services

Marine Logistics

We forward ship spares from suppliers to vessels — utilizing airfreight, seafreight, courier, hand-carry and arranging the delivery on board to even the most challenging destinations— ensuring vessels are always supplied without delay, in the most efficient and cost-effective way.

General Logistics

We manage the global transport of general cargo, providing the same level of efficiency, reliability, and tailored consulting to meet the unique needs of diverse industries. From small parcels to full truckloads, we ensure your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time.

Project Logistics

Our expertise extends to the management of complex and oversized cargo operations. We provide end-to-end planning and execution for specialized projects, ensuring the safe, reliable, and on-time transport of unique freight.

Warehousing & Digital Inventory Management

We provide storage in bonded and strategically positioned warehouses near major ports and airports, giving our clients a strong competitive advantage in speed and accessibility. Our digital system provides real-time tracking of stored items, fast preparation and dispatch as well as full transparency and control over every movement.

Customs and compliance

We offer comprehensive support for all customs and compliance procedures worldwide. Our services ensure seamless handling of all documentation and regulations, minimizing delays and reducing risk for your shipments.

Logistics Strategy & In-house Consulting

We provide hands-on support and advisory services to overcome challenges , meet critical deadlines, optimize logistics strategies, and reduce costs. We also act as your internal consultant, assisting with the establishment, training, and digitization of your in-house logistics department.
